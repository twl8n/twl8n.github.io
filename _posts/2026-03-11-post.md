#### Plant more trees

Every year we so a small order of bare root trees from the Virginia Department of Forestry. This year we got 5 Norway Spruce and 5 Scotch Pine. We plant them as a wind barrier, but after a few years we have accidentally created a Christmas tree farm. The Norway Spruce are **so** small.

I have to put out spare exectric fence posts to mark them so I don't mow over them later.

![](/image/IMG_6099.JPG)

We have had a few hot days, so I need to water the little trees until their roots are established. A big tub for the water, and the zero turn with a garden cart to move it around. I can easily haul more than 10 gallons of water.
